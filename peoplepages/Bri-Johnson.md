Bri Johnson is originally from Watertown, SD, and is an undergraduate student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. She is pursuing a degree in Forensic Science with minors in Chemistry and Criminal Justice. Bri joined the Schnable Lab in May 2026 to gain hands-on research experience and further develop her scientific skills. Outside of her academic and research interests, Bri is actively involved in campus leadership and service. She serves as a committee head for the Forensic Science Club, is the secretary of the CASNR Student Advisory Board, and volunteers with the Husker Pantry. She is excited to be working in the lab and contribute to ongoing research projects.
