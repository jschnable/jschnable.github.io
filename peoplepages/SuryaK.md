Surya Krishna Sakthivel is a Ph.D. student in Complex Biosystems at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln and is currently completing a lab rotation in the Schnable Lab. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in Agriculture and his Master’s degree in Genetics and Plant Breeding from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, India. His research interests include quantitative and comparative genomics, the effects of domestication and selection on agronomically important traits, and the development of genomic resources for crop improvement. Outside the lab, Surya enjoys traveling, hiking, and exploring different cuisines.
