Rossy Bedolla joined the Schnable Lab as Office Associate on July 1st, 2024. Since starting her career, she has gained extensive experience as Assistant and project coordinator, supporting research initiatives by managing documentation, organizing meetings, and liaising with stakeholders. Rossy now coordinates scientific projects, ensures smooth communication, and manages lab resources. She holds a Master’s in MBA with a concentration in Project Management from Instituto Europeo de Posgrado - IEP and a Bachelor’s in Biochemical Engineering from Universidad Autónoma de Sinaloa. Her dedication to fostering collaboration and ensuring the success of research projects is a testament to her commitment to advancing scientific discovery.
