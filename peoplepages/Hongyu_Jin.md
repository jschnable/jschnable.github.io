Hongyu Jin joined the Schnable Lab in 2019 as an Integrated Plant Biology PhD student
co-advised by Prof. Schnable and [Prof. Jinliang Yang](https://jyanglab.com/). He earned
his bachelor's degree from China Agricultural University before starting graduate
training, completed his PhD in August 2024, and then spent six months as a postdoctoral
researcher working with Prof. Schnable before departing for a new position in China
at the end of 2024.
